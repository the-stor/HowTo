Drop Biscuits and Sausage Gravy

Ingredients

Biscuit Ingredients

  • 3 C All Purpose Flour
  • 2 T Baking Powder
  • 1/2 t Salt
  • 1-1/2 Sticks of Cold Butter, cut up into pieces
  • 1-1/4 C Buttermilk

Sausage Gravy Ingredients

  • 1 Lb Breakfast Sausage
  • 1/3 C All Purpose Flour
  • 4 C Milk
  • 1/2 t Seasoned Salt
  • 2 t Black Pepper

Instructions

Biscuits Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Add the flour, baking powder, and salt to the mixing bowl of a food processor(or a large bowl.) Add the butter pieces and pulse until the butter is completely cut into the flour mixture(or use a pastry cutter if using a bowl.) While pulsing(or stirring) drizzle in the buttermilk util the dough just comes together and is no longer crumbly
  3. Drop in clumps on 2 baking sheets, then bake for 15-17 minutes or until golden brown.(Optional: Brush with melted butter when biscuits first come out of the oven.)

Sausage Gravy Instructions

  1. With your finger, tear small pieces of sausage and add them in a single layer to a large heavy skillet. Brown the sausage over medium-high heat until no longer pink. Reduce the heat to medium-low. Sprinkle on half the flour and stir so that the sausage soaks it all up, then add a little more until just before the sausage looks too dry. Stir it around and cook it for another minute or so, then pour in the milk, stirring constantly.
  2. Cook the gravy, stirring frequently, until it thickens.(This may take a good 10-12 minutes.) Sprinkle in the seasoned salt and pepper and continue cooking until very thick and luscious. If it gets too thick too soon, just splash in 1/2 cup of milk or more if needed. Taste and adjust seasonings.
  3. Spoon sausage gravy over warm biscuits and serve immediately!

Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Lasagna noodles
  • 2 lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 32 oz Spaghetti Sauce
  • 16 oz Ricotta Cheese
  • 2-1/4 C Mozzarella Cheese
  • 3/4 C Grated Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Soak 9 sheets of lasagna noodles in VERY hot water
  2. Meanwhile, brown beef. Drain fat and add onion then saut√© for an additional 2 minutes
  3. Add the sauce and ricotta cheese and heat thoroughly
  4. In a 13 by 9 inch pan, ladle enough sauce to thinly cover the bottom layer with 3 lasagna noodles, 2 cups of suace, 3/4 cups mozzararella cheese and 1/4 parmesan cheese, repeat twice
  5. Bake covered at 350 degrees F for 45-55 minutes

Aunt Bunny's Hobo Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Diced Onion
  • 1/2 lb Bacon
  • 1 C Ketchup
  • 1 C Brown Sugar
  • 1 Can of Pork and Beans
  • 1 Can of Kidney Beans
  • 1 Can of Butter Beans
  • 1 T Vinegar
  • 1 T Yellow Mustard

Instructions

  1. Fry the meat, with the onion and bacon; drain
  2. Drain kidney beans and butter beans
  3. Mix beans together and add ketchup, brown sugar, mustard and vinegar
  4. Add meat to the rest of the mixture and mix well
  5. Bake covered for one hour at 350 degrees F
